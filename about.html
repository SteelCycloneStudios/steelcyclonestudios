What We Offer

     Founded in 2012, Steel Cyclone Studios provides high quality custom apparel, shoes, merchandise and video games. Providing opportunities to all; especially minority students, Steel Cyclone Studios also offers a diverse range of affordable courses that encompasses a tremendous amount of learning material and lectures taught from actual practitioners. Our courses features a variety of topics including finincial education & literacy not taught in traditional schools and universities. Build your foundations in the stock market and learn how to find profitable trades! Overcome your struggles and create additional income streams by learning a wide range of side-hustles. Hone your skills and master your craftsmanship learning Photography, Website Development, Game Desgin and Programming entirely for FREE courtesy of 3DBUZZ.


Our Story

     Currently a small business ran by Jordon McClain, Steel Cyclone Studios aims to inspire players through inspirational clothing and soon... awesome indie games currently in development. As both a gamer and developer, I strive to create an experience that empowers and gives voice to players while closing the gap on what consumers want in the market. To get a better feel for what consumers want, I am committed to providing updates along with a behind -the-scenes look at the challenges I face in creating games. I invite you to join my extraordinary journey from code to controller. Feel free to browse the website and check out STEEL WEAR on the Store page for some awesome merchandise and sweet deals. If you like what I do please subscribe to my social media! Many thanks to all of my fans and supporters!


Vision & Mission

     Pushing the limits of what art means to the world, Steel Cyclone Studios is dedicated to bringing together gamers and the endless passionate energy of the motivated entrepreneur through gaming and apparel.

     Our games not only are fun filled action adventures with elements of comedy. Our games promote positive messages. We tell stories about good overcoming evil. We explore themes like friendship, self-sacrifice and fighting for the greater good. We create games and apparel that has a positive lasting influence on people's lives.

     We are the underdogs, on a mission to create unforgettable worlds, raise the bar with our craftsmanship and to give back and help the less fortunate by supporting worthwhile causes. We lead by example by sharing our knowledge and resources with the world to help empower others on their journey into game development. Furthermore, we are unafraid to share the challenges we face in the making of our games, our stories and topics of fairness.


Personal Message

     Personal Message: There is more to life than the shoes, clothes, and other material things you may see on this website. I promote the empowerment of educating yourself, living a drug free lifestyle, and seeking positive improvement daily. It is okay to treat yourself to material things if and only if you have all of your priorities in order. You should never under any circumstances short your bills or do anything to degrade your integrity for any material items especially shoes. At the end of the day they are only shoes, clothes, and other things that have no value on who you are as a person. Always be who you are and never follow unless you chose to follow GOD, bless!
